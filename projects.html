  • Northeastern Energy Flows
    Processing

    Northeastern Energy Flows is an interactive data visualization application built in Processing. NEF pulls data from units that track the energy usage of each building at Northeastern University, and displays each building's relative energy usage as its color on a map of Northeastern's Campus. NEF also allows users to view precise data over any 15 minute time interval, and visualize change in energy usage over time.
